The members

Jesse Rutherford

Rutherford was born in Thousand Oaks, California, on August 21, 1991. Ever since childhood
he has always been a part of the entertainment
industry because of which he had a passion towards doing songs and commercials. His first solo
project was released in 2011 with a
mixtape named "Truth Hurts, Truth Heals".In the same year
he connected with some local musicians with ideas of making a band. Thus, making
The neighbourhood, presenting a mix of atmospheric indie rock,
electronica, and hip-hop beats with melodic, R&B-influenced vocals.

Jeremy Freedman

Freedman started playing music after seeing his cousin's first guitar. He then soon asked his mother
for an acoustic guitar which he received next week. But as a huge heavy metal enthusiast,
he soon asked for an electric guitar.
Later he was in a metal band where Zach Abels and Mikey Margott were also in.
He said they the songs they produced while being in the band were horrible but Abels and Margott
just wanted to have fun.
Freedman's earliest influences were
David Gilmour, Tony Iommi, Jimmy Page, and The Beatles. During this time, he met Rutherford,
with whom he initially did few performances with, after which he became a part of the group

Zach Abels

Abels was born in Simi valley, california, on July 3, 1992. Zach is a guitarist for the rock band
The Neighbourhood whose best known for their debut album I Love You.. Abels and
his fellow band members came together in 2011, he and the band released a debut EP called
I'm Sorry... in May of 2012.

Mikey margott

Margott was born in california. He is the bassist of the band, Neighbourhood. He is also kown for
"The amazing spider-man 2 (2014)" and "The Equalizer (2014)".

Brandon Fried

Brandon was the second drummer for the Neighbourhood whose best
known for their debut album I Love You. Fried joined the band replacing the
former drummer Bryan Sammis. In November of 2022, Brandon was fired from the band
because of allegations of SA against him became public
